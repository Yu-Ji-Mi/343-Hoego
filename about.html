Jimi McDermott

I'm Jimi McDermott, and I'm currently a senior at James Madison University. Throughout my time at JMU, I've found a unique balance between my love for video games and film photography. As a Computer Science major, I've delved deep into the digital realm, where I've not only honed my coding skills but also indulged my passion for video games. For me, gaming is not just a pastime; it's a source of inspiration that has influenced my academic journey and fueled my fascination with the ever-evolving digital landscape.

Beyond the digital realm, I find solace in the world of film photography. The art of capturing moments through the lens of an analog camera, the anticipation of developing film, and the timeless quality of each photograph hold a special place in my heart. This dual fascination with the digital and analog spheres keeps me creatively engaged, always seeking new perspectives and ways to express myself. Alongside my academic and creative pursuits, I'm an active member of Greek life, where I've formed lasting bonds, embraced leadership opportunities, and found a sense of community. As a senior, I'm excited to savor the final moments of my college experience, continuing to explore the vast digital and photographic landscapes that intrigue me. College has been a journey of self-discovery and personal growth, and I can't wait to see where my passions take me next.
